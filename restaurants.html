About the restaurant

"Top of the chain" - a project by Yerikov Ulan and Davlat Ushurbakiev. A signature cuisine restaurant that practices a conscious approach to gastronomy. City dwellers want everything to be fair in the restaurant - a memorable interior that can be found on both sides of the Atlantic Ocean, signature cocktails that are not served anywhere else, and interesting food professionally prepared exclusively from quality products.

Kitchen

We offer the concept of comfortable food, which gives a person a feeling of comfort and happiness. The menu is based on original recipes with an emphasis on the most understandable, high-quality, seasonal products. All products in our kitchen are made with our own hands - from yeast-free bread to meat gastronomy, and one of the main features of the kitchen is cooking over an open fire.

Bar and wine

A real bar is a reflection of the life of the city and its attractions. As an integral part of micro-urbanism, we have dedicated the bar card to our city, its landmark, historical events and great works that have shaped the urban culture. The menu includes 18 signature cocktails, which were developed by our brand barman Nursat Berikbayev. The drinks are made only from natural, seasonal ingredients.

Chef's table

A fascinating gastronomic world where live communication with the chef takes place at the community table. Chef's table is a theme-specific dinner set, accompanied by a perfectly matched wine for each course. This is an opportunity to visit Copenhagen, New York or London without leaving the center of Almaty.
